About Us

Welcome to ST AUTO, your one-stop destination for all your car needs. We're dedicated to providing you the very best of car detailing services, with a focus on dependability, customer service, and uniqueness.

Founded in 2024 by car enthusiast Saurabh Thorat, ST AUTO has come a long way from its beginnings in a small garage. We now serve customers all over the world and are thrilled to be a part of the ever-growing eco-friendly car care industry.

We hope you enjoy our products and services as much as we enjoy offering them to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us at stauto@gmail.com
